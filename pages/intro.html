What is this system about?

The following system allows to help you make decisions for your organization. Depending on your previous efforts, you start with a certain number of tokens. This allows you to vote with a certain amount of voting weight.

Making a proposal

How money is moved

When you login you will be able to vote. When this vote passes a certain threshold the funds will be dispersed automatically to beneficiary.
